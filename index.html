Dubai is awesome!
Dubai is a city of skyscrapers, ports, and beaches, where big business takes place alongside sun-seeking tourism.

Explore Dubai

Visit Burj Khalifa, the tallest man-made building in the world. With astonishing 828m. While you are at it, Dubai Mall and Dubai fountain are just beneath.
Get on top of Dubai Frame, a building that divides Old & New Dubai and experince the time warp, represented by the Frame.
Explore Palm Jumeirah, the largest man-made island in the world. Enjoy yourself in Aquaventure Waterpark, or stop by for a coffee at The Pointe.
One of Dubai's most famous landmarks, the Museum of the Future. The museum explores how society could evolve in the coming decades using science and technology
